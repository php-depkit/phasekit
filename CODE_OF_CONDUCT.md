# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, but do not avoid direct technical feedback.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in removal from discussions or contribution channels.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to the maintainers through a private channel when possible.
